Some Characters From the Game

Amber

Pyro • Bow • Outrider of the Knights of Favonius

Always energetic and optimistic, Amber glides across Mondstadt skies and never hesitates to help those in need.

Barbara

Hydro • Catalyst • Deaconess & Idol

Mondstadt’s shining idol, healing allies with her songs and Hydro vision.

Bennett

Pyro • Sword • Adventurer

A boy with notoriously bad luck, yet he always charges ahead with a smile.

Diluc

Pyro • Claymore • Darknight Hero

The owner of Dawn Winery, protecting Mondstadt from the shadows with his blazing claymore.

Fischl

Electro • Bow • Investigator

A mysterious girl who travels with Oz, speaking of worlds beyond Teyvat.

Jean

Anemo • Sword • Acting Grand Master

Ever reliable, Jean devotes herself to maintaining peace in Mondstadt.

Kaeya

Cryo • Sword • Cavalry Captain

A charming yet secretive captain whose sharp wit hides many mysteries.

Klee

Pyro • Catalyst • Spark Knight

Mondstadt’s tiny explosives expert, always ready with another “boom”.

Lisa

Electro • Catalyst • Librarian

The Knights of Favonius librarian, as laid-back as she is intelligent.

Noelle

Geo • Claymore • Maid

A hardworking maid who dreams of becoming a full Knight someday.

Razor

Electro • Claymore • Wolf Boy

Raised by wolves in Wolvendom, Razor fights with fierce instincts and a giant sword.

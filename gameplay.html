Gameplay in Genshin Impact

Explore, fight, glide, climb, unlock powers, defeat bosses, and discover a vast open world.

Different Gameplay Styles

Open World Exploration Map

Climb mountains, glide across landscapes, swim in rivers, and uncover hidden secrets.

Elemental Combat

Combine seven elements such as Pyro, Hydro, Electro, Cryo, Geo, Anemo, and Dendro for powerful reactions.

Boss Battles

Face giant enemies with unique attack patterns and claim valuable rewards.

Puzzle Solving

Solve elemental puzzles, unlock hidden chests, and activate ancient mechanisms.

Gliding & Movement

Use wind currents, gliders, and stamina to travel across massive landscapes.

Co-Op Multiplayer

Join friends to explore Teyvat together, battle bosses, and complete domains.
